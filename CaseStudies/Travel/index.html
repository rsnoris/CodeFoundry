CASE STUDY

Travel & Hospitality Use Cases

CodeFoundry partners with hospitality and travel brands to delight customers and optimize operations with next-gen platforms, smart automation, and data-driven guest experiences.

1. End-to-End Online Booking System
A hotel chain modernized with a cloud-native booking engine and unified inventory across all direct and indirect channels.
  • Custom website and mobile apps with live pricing and room status.
  • API connection to GDS, OTAs, and travel partners.
  • Automated booking/confirmation and easy up-sell opportunities.
2. AI-powered Revenue & Yield Optimization
A hospitality company used CodeFoundry’s ML models to maximize revenue and dynamic pricing by property, room, or season.
  • Real-time demand forecasting from market, event, and seasonality data.
  • Automated repricing and promotion across channels.
  • Increased RevPAR (Revenue per Available Room) by 11% in year one.
3. Personalized Guest Mobile App
A global brand delivered a mobile guest app for check-in/out, loyalty, mobile key, services ordering, and local info.
  • Integration with PMS, door lock, POS, and loyalty backend.
  • Personalized offers, local experiences, and feedback forms.
  • Boosted app engagement and cross-sells/tips.
4. Smart Facility and Room Automation
Hotel group rolled out IoT-powered energy control, predictive maintenance, and guest-driven room automation (lighting, HVAC).
  • Sensors and in-room tablets for guest controls and feedback.
  • Real-time service engineer alerting for detected issues.
  • Energy cost savings and improved guest satisfaction scores.
5. Guest Analytics Dashboard for Personalization
Group-wide analytics synced guest journeys across website, app, and on-property touchpoints for personalization and service recovery.
  • Unified guest profile and stay history integration with CRM.
  • AI clustering of guest types and high-value segment identification.
  • Automated personalized offers, upgrades, and communications.
6. Digital Concierge and Chatbots
Resort chain introduced AI-powered chatbots for 24/7 guest inquiry, bookings, room service, and local recommendations.
  • Multi-language support, handover to live agent when needed.
  • Reduced call center volume by >30%.
  • Seamless workflow from digital to on-property staff.
7. Centralized Feedback and Reputation Management
Hotel group consolidated guest reviews and feedback from all major online and on-site channels for proactive response and service improvement.
  • Sentiment analysis and smart alerts for negative comments.
  • Workflow for service recovery and escalation tracking.
  • Systematic improvement of review scores and increased bookings.
