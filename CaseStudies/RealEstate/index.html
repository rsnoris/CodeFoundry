CASE STUDY

Real Estate Use Cases

CodeFoundry empowers real estate firms with PropTech innovation, digital workflow, analytics, and mobile engagement from leasing to property IoT.

1. PropTech Cloud Platform for Listings
A commercial agency unified all property listings and analytics in a single white-label cloud platform for brokers and clients.
  • API import of third-party and legacy listings; AI tagging of properties.
  • Role-based dashboards for brokers and investors.
  • Client sharing, digital tours, and lead management built in.
2. Smart Leasing Workflow Automation
A multifamily group streamlined the lease process, reducing time to close and back office cost by nearly half.
  • Automated ID check, credit analysis, and e-signature flows.
  • Digital document storage and integrated move-in checklist app.
  • Automated notifications and reminders for tenants and staff.
3. Mobile Tenant Experience Portal
Ground-up development of a single app and portal for tenants: maintenance requests, payments, visitor access and communications.
  • Integrated with building access control and smart home devices.
  • Push notifications for package arrivals and community events.
  • User feedback loop improved NPS and renewals.
4. AI-Driven Property Analytics
A real estate fund manager launched a new dashboard providing predictive analytics on pricing, demand, and lease risk by region.
  • Data integration from market, census, and brokerage systems.
  • ML models for pricing, vacancy risk, tenant churn, and maintenance forecasting.
  • Role-based ROI calculators for acquisitions and renovation planning.
5. Virtual Tours and 3D Visualization
A residential firm wanted immersive digital showings for remote buyers during the pandemic.
  • 360° tours using camera, lidar, and drone imaging.
  • Self-service virtual open house scheduling with VR/AR option.
  • Embedded in mobile apps and websites.
6. Contract Automation with Blockchain
A commercial REIT reduced legal overhead and disputes by introducing blockchain-based smart contracts for lease and sale agreements.
  • Immutable digital contracts, signed and executed on blockchain.
  • Automated payment flows and penalty triggers.
  • Compliance reporting and audit trails auto-generated.
7. IoT-enabled Building Operations
Office developer enabled smart energy, access, and analytics across properties.
  • Connected thermostats, lighting, and security to central management cloud.
  • Cut utility costs 18%; reduced unauthorized access events by >70%.
  • Automated notifications for preventive maintenance and anomaly detection.
8. Digital Marketing and Lead Management
A brokerage automated its outreach and lead nurture with integrated digital marketing and analytics.
  • Smart emailing, social ads, and audience targeting based on browsing, CRM and campaign analytics.
  • Lead scoring for sales, rental, and investment teams.
  • AI-powered property matching for fast, accurate recommendations.
