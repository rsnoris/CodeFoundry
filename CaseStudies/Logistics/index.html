CASE STUDY

Logistics Use Cases

CodeFoundry unlocks smart logistics with IoT, real-time tracking, route optimization, and connected digital supply chain experiences for global shippers and carriers.

1. Real-time Shipment Tracking
A logistics firm deployed IoT sensors and cloud-based tracking to give customers a real-time view of all shipments and assets.
  • GPS, temperature, and shock monitoring for every container or pallet.
  • Customer portal with live progress maps and automated ETAs.
  • Historical route and delay analysis for SLA improvement.
2. AI-powered Route and Load Optimization
A fleet needed to minimize miles, fuel usage, and late deliveries across thousands of daily trips.
  • Dynamic route optimization engine (dispatch + in-transit replan) for cost, traffic, fuel, and weather.
  • Load balancing based on live and predicted orders, vehicle size, and legal restrictions.
  • Cut empty miles by 22% and late deliveries by 37%.
3. Warehouse Automation and Analytics
A regional logistics player modernized its warehouses with automation and real-time analytics.
  • Automated guided vehicles (AGV), barcoding, and digital pick lists.
  • Live inventory dashboards and predictive reordering.
  • Labor efficiency optimization reduced overtime by 15%.
4. Cross-border Compliance Automation
An international logistics company adopted digital customs and compliance workflows.
  • Automated trade documents, declarations, and duties calculation for 40+ countries.
  • Integrated OCR and ML APIs for document scanning and error detection.
  • Dropped compliance delays by 81% for cross-border shipments.
5. Booking API for Partner Ecosystem
A 3PL needed to digitize orders and orchestration for its network of carriers and retailers.
  • RESTful booking API, SOAP integration with legacy partner systems.
  • Real-time and batch order submission with tracking updates.
  • 360° order visibility for shippers, consignees, and agents.
6. Fleet Management Telematics
A trucking company gained real-time insight into each vehicle and driver for insurance and safety.
  • Onboard telematics for speed, braking, hours, and fault codes.
  • Automated service reminders, regulatory compliance alerts (ELD, HOS).
  • Reduced accident risk scores by 29%.
7. Cold-chain Management Optimization
A perishables logistics firm built a system to track and alert for temperature excursions in real-time.
  • IoT temperature, humidity, and shock sensors on all cold-chain shipments.
  • Immediate SMS/Push alerts for at-risk products, shelf-life predictions.
  • Reduced spoilage/waste rates by 19%.
8. Returns and Reverse Logistics Platform
A retailer and shipper needed a digital reverse logistics platform to optimize cost and customer experience for returns.
  • Customer-initiated returns with smart routing to nearest warehouse/repair.
  • Automated refund and credit tracking linked with e-commerce and ERP.
  • Reduced average return-to-stock cycle by 42%.
