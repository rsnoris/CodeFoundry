CASE STUDY

Manufacturing Use Cases

CodeFoundry drives digital transformation for manufacturing: smart factories, IoT automation, supply chain innovation, and operational analytics for efficiency and resilience.

1. IoT Smart Factory Deployment
A global manufacturer automated oversight of 20+ plants by rolling out IoT sensors and edge devices.
  • Real-time telemetry for equipment status, power, and safety.
  • Automated alert system for preventive maintenance.
  • Reduced unplanned downtime by 28% and overall maintenance cost by $2.5M/year.
2. Predictive Maintenance with AI
A plant owner wanted to reduce major failures in bottling and assembly machines.
  • Trained ML models on historical failures and live sensor streams.
  • Maintenance suggestions auto-queued to service teams before breakdowns occurred.
  • Saw drop in catastrophic failure events to near zero within 12 months.
3. MES and ERP Integration
Disconnected manufacturing execution systems (MES) needed linking to main ERP for end-to-end visibility on jobs, resources, and inventory.
  • Custom API bridge for real-time sync of job status, material movement, and waste.
  • Automated inventory triggers for under-stocked or overrun items.
  • Increased OEE (Overall Equipment Effectiveness) and production throughput.
4. Quality Automation with Computer Vision
A food packager replaced sample-based human inspection with 99.7% accurate, AI-powered quality assurance.
  • Installed real-time, high-def cameras at checkpoints in packaging line.
  • Live computer vision flagged defects and removed bad units without human input.
  • Defects detected before shipment increased by 4x.
5. Robotics Workflow and MES Data Analytics
A hardware manufacturer used a new analytics dashboard to optimize robotic workflows and operator efficiency.
  • Cross-referenced sensor, MES, and operator shift data for performance optimization.
  • Visualized causes of downtime and success rate of robots vs. manual intervention.
  • Boosted efficiency of robotic work cells by 18%.
6. Smart Supply Chain and Vendor Collaboration
A multi-vendor global supply chain improved collaboration through secure APIs and shared dashboards.
  • Real-time dashboards of logistics, inventory, and order status for all supply chain partners.
  • Automated notifications of late shipments, shortages, and overages via email/SMS/API.
  • Lowered end-to-end supply delays by 16% and improved partner satisfaction.
7. Regulatory Compliance & Traceability
Food & beverage client needed full product traceability for recall and compliance with FDA standards.
  • Track and record every batch and material movement, from raw intake to end product packaging.
  • Digitized lot recall and regulatory reporting in a single click.
8. Energy Management Optimization
CodeFoundry enabled energy metering across a facility, surfacing inefficiencies and saving costs.
  • Automated real-time alerts for excessive power drain or outages.
  • Historical trend analysis for off-peak load scheduling.
  • Result: Lowered energy cost by 23% over the first year.
