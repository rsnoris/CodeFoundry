CASE STUDY

Retail & E-commerce Use Cases

Modern commerce is omnichannel and global. CodeFoundry helps retailers embrace digital-first strategies, AI, and analytics for seamless and profitable customer experiences.

1. Omnichannel Shopping Platform for Fashion Retailer
A leading apparel chain wanted to “unify” its e-commerce and in-store channels for faster fulfillment and personalized marketing.
  • Single cart, profile, and loyalty program synced across web, app, and POS.
  • Cloud-based real-time inventory visibility for all channels.
  • AI-powered product recommendations and promotions.
  • Result: 35% YoY growth in digital sales and 50% increase in click/reserve at store.
2. Advanced Personalization for D2C Brands
A digital retailer wanted to offer data-driven, hyper-personalized experiences across its site and app.
  • Integrated real-time analytics with customer 360-degree profiles.
  • Automated triggered campaigns based on lifecycle stage.
  • Dynamic segmentation for high-value targeting and recommendations.
3. Scalability for Black Friday/Cyber Monday Events
A global e-commerce brand faced flash traffic spikes each season. CodeFoundry deployed auto-scaling, CDN, and cache patterns to support 1M+ concurrent users with zero downtime.
  • Containerized app services on AWS ECS with autoscale triggers.
  • Smart image optimization, prefetching and edge caching dropped page loads by 52%.
  • Post-mortem review and tuning after each peak event.
4. AI Visual Search for Online Catalogs
An e-commerce platform expanded user engagement by letting customers search for products with images, not just keywords.
  • Custom vision AI model trained on SKUs and 3rd-party supplier images.
  • Mobile app “snap & shop” visual search API.
  • Increased conversion rates for mobile shoppers by 19%.
5. Next-Gen POS Integration for Brick & Click
A retailer replaced siloed legacy POS with a unified cloud POS solution, enabling returns/cross-store pickup and omni-customer service.
  • Modern REST API integration with ERP, CRM, and e-commerce.
  • Mobile checkout, curbside pickup, and digital receipt in one workflow.
  • Fraud detection on POS with ML for returns and credit cards.
6. Inventory AI for Demand Forecasting
A retailer improved turn rate and profit by forecasting demand and adjusting logistics in real time.
  • ML models ingesting seasonality, channel, and external factors.
  • Automatic order suggestion for hot SKUs and sell-down optimization before end-of-life.
  • Reduced deadstock by 24%.
7. Loyalty & Rewards Engine Platform
A group of connected brands required a scalable rewards platform for all e-commerce sites and in-store, with gamification and referral tracking.
  • Microservices loyalty platform exposed via API.
  • Personalization for reward options and balances.
  • Referred friend conversion tracking with seamless sign-up.
