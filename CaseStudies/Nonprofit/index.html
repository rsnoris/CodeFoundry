CASE STUDY

Nonprofit & Social Impact Use Cases

CodeFoundry accelerates social good for nonprofits and NGOs with digital transformation, donor engagement, data transparency, and optimized service delivery.

1. Digital Donation & Crowdfunding Platform
A global NGO needed an easy-to-use donation portal, with campaigns, recurring giving, and peer-to-peer fundraising.
  • Mobile, web, and social integration (Apple/Google Pay, JustGiving, Facebook, etc).
  • Automated receipts, gift-matching workflows, and real-time campaign dashboards.
  • Increased donations, supporter retention, and global reach.
2. Impact Analytics & Outcomes Dashboard
A foundation wanted to rigorously track program results and impact with custom dashboards and data visualization.
  • Data ingestion from partners, surveys, field staff apps, sensed data (IoT where relevant).
  • Automated visualization and real-time alerts for KPIs/goals by region and program.
  • Published progress and compliance reports for donors, boards, and government.
3. Field Operative Mobile Platform
A humanitarian nonprofit launched an offline/low-bandwidth mobile solution for medical and logistics teams.
  • Case reporting, GIS-map, inventory/order apps with offline sync and auto-upload.
  • Security and data privacy for at-risk locations and teams.
  • Real-time coordination with headquarters and donors for urgent needs.
4. Volunteer Matching & Scheduling
A citywide nonprofit coalition digitalized volunteer intake, onboarding, training, and event scheduling.
  • Mobile/desktop app for sign-up, credentials, shifts, training, and reminders.
  • Automated match-making for location, skill, and interest; feedback and reward system built in.
5. Grant Management & Automated Reporting
A grant-making organization moved to a fully digital workflow for proposal submission, review, awards, and reporting.
  • Role-based submission portal, guided applications, status tracking.
  • Milestone analytics, reporting and documentation flows, payment triggers on approval.
6. Online Education/Resource Library
A literacy nonprofit created a digital hub for curriculum, e-learning, and resource sharing with parents and teachers worldwide.
  • Multilingual content, video, quizzes; user management and progress tracking.
  • Offline access, print/PDF downloads, and API for third-party integration.
7. Transparency, Anti-Fraud & Compliance Automation
A large international relief organization integrated digital audits, payment traceability, and anti-bribery controls.
  • Blockchain receipt logging for donations, supplies, and vendor payments.
  • Automated compliance and local law reporting.
