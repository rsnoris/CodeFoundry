CASE STUDY

Healthcare Use Cases

CodeFoundry helps healthcare organizations deliver safe, compliant, modern digital patient experiences, analytics, and operational excellence at enterprise and clinical scale.

1. Unified EHR Migration & Modernization
A large hospital network needed to migrate 500K+ patient records from legacy systems to a scalable, interoperable electronic health record (EHR) cloud, all while meeting HIPAA and HITRUST requirements.
  • Data cleansing, mapping, and transfer to a cloud-native EHR with HL7/FHIR support.
  • Single patient identifier provided to all departments, reducing double entry and errors by >60%.
  • Real-time integrations with labs, pharmacies, and insurance clearinghouses.
2. HIPAA-Compliant Telehealth Platform
A regional provider expanded remote care with a cloud-based telemedicine solution.
  • Encrypted video, chat, and eRX integrated into EHR workflow.
  • Custom patient mobile app for scheduling and virtual visits.
  • Automated reporting for CMS and payer compliance.
3. Predictive Analytics for Patient Readmission
A university hospital sought to decrease costly patient readmissions and improve outcomes through data.
  • Deployed scalable data lakes and ML models to flag at-risk discharges and suggest interventions.
  • Integrated with nurse/staff workflow.
  • Reduced readmission index by 17% after 12 months.
4. Interoperable Imaging & PACS in the Cloud
A multi-location hospital required a cloud-native Picture Archival and Communication System (PACS) with fast, secure access.
  • Migrated 1.8M+ images with zero patient data loss.
  • Role-based and location-aware access controls.
  • Supports vendor-neutral archive for future flexibility.
5. Population Health Dashboard for Public Health
Partnered with a health district to build an advanced dashboard tracking epidemiological trends during and after the pandemic.
  • Real-time data ingestion from hospitals, clinics, and third-party data sources.
  • Powerful role-based analytics and drilldown for researchers and administrators.
  • Automated reporting of outbreaks and compliance metrics.
6. Mobile Health (mHealth) App Suite
A pediatric system needed a secure mobile health (mHealth) app suite for families.
  • HIPAA-compliant appointment, vaccination, and telehealth reminders.
  • In-app symptom checker and nurse chat.
  • Push notification and direct messaging integrated with EHR.
7. Automated Claims & Billing Integration
A hospital finance team automated error-prone billing processes to reduce denials and speed up claims.
  • API integration with major insurers (EDI/X12 837 & 835 support).
  • Automatic checks for common errors and missing data before submission.
  • 50% reduction in manual work, >97% clean claim rate.
8. Secure Messaging for Care Coordination
A home health group needed to securely share plans and patient updates across agencies, reducing fragmentation.
  • Role-based secure messaging replacing faxes and phone.
  • Audit logging for compliance and best practice tracking.
  • Result: improved speed and satisfaction for clinical teams and families.
