CASE STUDY

Aerospace & Defense Use Cases

CodeFoundry advances aerospace and defense organizations with mission-critical software, IoT, AI, and supply chain innovation.

1. Digital Twin for Fleet Management
A global airline and defense contractor deployed digital twins for aircraft, engines, and major mechanical subsystems.
  • Integrates real-time sensor/telemetry, historical maintenance, and operational context.
  • Predicts failures, optimizes maintenance, improves availability and safety.
  • 10% reduction in grounded days across all tracked aircraft.
2. Mission Planning & Scheduling Automation Platform
A space agency required an intelligent, collaborative platform for complex mission timeline and asset scheduling.
  • Gantt-based interface, resource AI, and multi-agency access controls.
  • Real-time alerts and workflow for approval of changes and conflicts.
3. Secure Supply Chain & Vendor Risk Analytics
A defense prime gained new visibility and resilience in its multivendor global supply chain.
  • Automated onboarding, data-sharing, and risk scoring for all suppliers.
  • Proactive disruption alerting (geopolitics, capacity, sanctions, cyber, IP risk).
  • Improved compliance and audit outcomes.
4. Satellite Ground Station Automation
A newspace operator rolled out cloud-native automation for satellite tracking, data ingest, and command & control.
  • Remote scheduling and contact sequencing for rapid re-tasking.
  • Telemetry and control APIs for integration with mission operations and customers.
5. Predictive Quality Assurance & Defect Analytics
An aerospace manufacturer deployed AI-powered vision systems for in-line part inspection and end-to-end traceability.
  • Automated defect detection at ultrafine part and subassembly levels.
  • Dashboard for root cause analysis and process improvements across plants.
6. Secure Collaboration & Classified Content Management
A defense program deployed a compliant, role-based file and communication platform for joint government-industry work.
  • Document & messaging platform with multi-factor, clearance-aware access controls.
  • Full audit logging, export control, and end-to-end encryption.
7. Remote Monitoring of Critical Ground Assets
Defense agency employed IoT/edge and satellite for 24/7 monitoring of radar, comms, and sensitive field equipment globally.
  • Near real-time streaming, health status analytics, and auto alerting to command operations.
  • Offline/remote patching and diagnostics enabled via secure links.
