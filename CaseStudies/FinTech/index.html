CASE STUDY

FinTech & Banking Use Cases

CodeFoundry partners with financial leaders to deliver secure, innovative, and scalable digital solutions across the FinTech landscape. Explore real-world scenarios below.

1. PCI DSS Payment Gateway for Neo-Bank
A digital-only challenger bank needed to rapidly launch in multiple markets. CodeFoundry designed a PCI DSS-compliant payment gateway supporting instant onboarding, modern card issuing, and real-time anti-fraud screening.
  • Built a scalable microservices architecture on AWS for transaction routing and ledgering.
  • Integrated with MasterCard & Visa APIs for instant card issuance.
  • Real-time AI-powered fraud monitoring reduced chargebacks by 73%.
  • Achieved >99.995% uptime through active-active, multi-region failover and automated healing.
2. API Platform for Open Banking
A major bank required an open-banking platform to enable safe fintech integrations while maintaining strict regulatory controls.
  • Developed an OAuth2-secured API suite for third-party access to accounts and payments per PSD2.
  • Deployed sandbox and production environments with audit trails and API analytics for partners.
  • Onboarded 200+ fintech partners with strong onboarding SDKs and documentation.
3. Identity & Biometric Authentication Upgrade
A global payment network needed to modernize security without degrading the user experience.
  • Added biometric (facial & fingerprint) authentication support to mobile apps and ATMs.
  • Integrated with FIDO2/WebAuthn standard for passwordless login flow.
  • Reduced account takeover incidents by 80% in the first year.
4. Algorithmic Trading Platform Modernization
An institutional asset manager needed real-time AI and analytics for algorithmic trading at scale.
  • Re-architected on Azure's Kubernetes Service for low-latency, high-frequency execution.
  • Integrated real-time data feeds with big data analytics for ML-based trading decisions.
  • Enabled client access to custom reporting and dashboards via white-label SaaS portal.
5. Digital Lending & Loan Marketplace
A fast-growing fintech startup wanted an integrated loan origination and marketplace platform.
  • Built a cloud-native, multi-tenant SaaS platform connecting borrowers and lenders.
  • Automated KYC, credit scoring, document workflow, and digital signatures.
  • Seamless integration with multiple credit bureaus and payment rails (ACH, SEPA, UPI).
6. Crypto Custody & Regulatory Compliance
A fintech innovator needed to launch digital asset custody services aligned to shifting global regulation.
  • Developed secure, multi-signature crypto custody with hardware security modules (HSM).
  • Implemented continuous KYC/AML and transaction monitoring for compliance in US & EU.
7. Real-time Alerts and Risk Management
To enhance compliance and customer experience, a retail bank leveraged streaming technology to proactively monitor for suspicious banking activity.
  • Integrated Kafka-based event streaming for instant fraud, limit and compliance monitoring.
  • Automated customer alert engines for rapid response to flagged actions.
8. Scaling KYC for Global App Expansion
A remittance fintech scaling globally needed robust, automated Know Your Customer flows for high-volume onboarding.
  • Orchestrated integrations with 40+ global KYC providers and digital ID verifiers.
  • Slashed manual reviews by 90% via ML-driven risk pipeline and OCR.
